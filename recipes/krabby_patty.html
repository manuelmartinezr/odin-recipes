Krabby Patty Recipe

Description

The Krabby Patty is the Krusty Krab's signature and popular food item. It is a meatless hamburger of sorts made from seaweed-sea buns, undersea vegetables, condiments, and a patty; it can also be ordered with cheese.

Ingredients

  • Seaweed-sea buns
  • Undersea vegetables
  • Condiments
  • A patty
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the crabmeat, sauteed onions and celery, breadcrumbs, Dijon mustard, mayonnaise, egg, salt, and pepper in a mixing bowl.
  2. Mix well until all ingredients are properly combined.
  3. Create patties from the mixture.
  4. Make round patty shapes using a mold or your hands.
